How Does Our Recruitment Process Work?

The recruitment process begins when candidates submit their applications through the website or email. The HR team then reviews and shortlists suitable candidates for interview rounds to assess their skills and fit. Successful applicants receive a job offer, and once accepted, they proceed to the onboarding stage to start their roles.
